About US

Vila JIOTA was born from a dream to create a peaceful retreat where travelers could experience the natural beauty of the Albanian coast while enjoying exceptional comfort and warm hospitality.

Tucked away in the heart of Ksamil, our villa blends harmoniously with its surroundings, offering guests a relaxing escape just steps from the turquoise waters and soft sandy beaches.

What began as a small family-run hideaway has grown into one of the area's most beloved destinations. Our passion for detail, commitment to quality, and dedication to making every guest feel at home are the heart of everything we do.
