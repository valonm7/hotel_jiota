About Vila JIOTA

Discover our story, our values, and the people who make your stay unforgettable

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Vila JIOTA began as a dream to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience the authentic beauty of the Albanian coast while enjoying world-class comfort and hospitality. Nestled in the picturesque bay of Vlorë, our villa was designed to blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings while offering modern luxury.

Over the years, we have grown from a small family-run establishment to one of the most sought-after destinations in the region. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and genuine care for our guests have earned us a reputation as a premier coastal retreat.

Today, Vila JIOTA continues to evolve while staying true to our original vision: providing an unforgettable experience where serenity meets luxury, where every guest feels at home, and where memories are created that last a lifetime.

Our Values

Genuine Hospitality

We believe in creating authentic connections with our guests, treating everyone who walks through our doors as family, and ensuring their comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.

Environmental Responsibility

We are committed to sustainable practices that preserve the natural beauty of our surroundings, minimize our ecological footprint, and contribute to the well-being of our planet.

Excellence in Service

We strive for perfection in every detail, continuously improving our offerings, and going above and beyond to exceed our guests' expectations at every touchpoint.
